What is Steampunk?

The term "Steampunk" was developed during the late 1900. This subgenre of science fiction encompasses the remarkable technological progession of the Victorian Era, where steam-powered machinery was becoming a dominant force in society, further evolving humanity's way of life and production. Steampunk can be explored through many areas of our culture, specifically literature, art, fashion, music, and more. We will be exploring how Steampunk makes its stand in the fashion industry, and can be found in ready-to-wear clothing and even couture at major fashion houses.
